How to Use the Invoice Command Center

1

Import your weekly Excel file

Drag and drop your batch spreadsheet. The app reads the CONT NO and INV# columns automatically. You can also type containers manually.

2

Copy the Claude Prompt

The app generates a ready-to-paste prompt with all your container and invoice numbers. Paste it into your Claude chat to have Claude download each invoice and POD from QuickBooks Online.

3

Upload the downloaded PDFs

Drag all downloaded files into the upload zone. The app auto-matches them to containers by filename. Use "Manual Assign" for any it can't match.

4

Merge & Download

Preview the merge order (Invoice → POD for each container), then click Merge. Files are renamed to Container#_invoice and Container#_POD automatically. The final PDF downloads as Merged_Invoices.pdf.

↓

Export a log

Click the download icon (↓) in the top bar to export a CSV log of this week's batch — useful for record-keeping.

🔒 Security Policy

• Claude operates in read-only mode inside QuickBooks Online — it can search and download only. It will never create, edit, delete, or submit any transactions.
• You log in yourself — Claude never handles your QuickBooks credentials or passwords.
• No financial data is stored — this app runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
• All downloaded files stay on your local machine.
